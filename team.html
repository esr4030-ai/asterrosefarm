Team

Stacey Thiele

Co-Founder & Owner

Stacey co-founded Aster Rose Farm with a vision to create a world-class boarding facility for serious equestrians. With a deep passion for the equestrian lifestyle and commitment to excellence, Stacey ensures every aspect of the farm reflects her dedication to providing discerning horse owners with premium care and facilities. Her leadership and vision have made Aster Rose Farm a premier destination for equestrian boarding in Wellington.

Johan Rosenberg

Co-Founder, Owner & Facilities Manager

Johan co-founded Aster Rose Farm, bringing decades of business expertise and entrepreneurial success to the operation. A family-centered professional with a love for horses, Johan oversees all day-to-day operations and facility management. His strategic vision and meticulous attention to detail have made Aster Rose Farm a premier destination for equestrian boarding. Johan ensures that all facilities are maintained to the highest standards, supported by a very skilled team of grounds keepers.

Sophie Rosenberg

Hunter Rider - 3 Foot

Sophie is an accomplished hunter rider competing at the 3-foot level. Her achievements in the hunter ring exemplify the quality of riders and horses that thrive at our facility. Sophie's competitive spirit and love for horses make her an integral part of the Aster Rose Farm family.
